Why is recycling so important?

Learn the importance and proper way to recycle!

Recycling not only prevents pollution but also reduce the waste going to our land and ocean.

Recycling can help reduce your carbon footprint, the need for harvesting raw materials, saves energy, reduces greenhouse gases, prevents pollution, and much more. We can help keep the environment clean and preserve our natural resources by improving our recycling habits.
